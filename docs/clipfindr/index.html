Everything you need to clip faster

Stop scrubbing timelines. Let AI do the heavy lifting.

Transcript Search

Type anything and instantly jump to every moment it was said in your videos. Powered by Whisper AI transcription.

Peak Detection

Automatically find the highest-energy moments in your footage. Great for highlights, reactions, and best-of reels.

One-Click Export

Queue up clips with precise start/end timestamps and export them all at once as MP4s. No timeline editing required.

Native Desktop App

Works completely offline on your own machine. No cloud uploads, no subscriptions — your footage stays private.

Multi-Video Library

Load as many videos as you want. Each one gets its own transcription and you can search across your whole session.

Instant Results

Debounced live search returns results as you type. Click a timestamp to jump directly to that moment in the video.
